Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Artificial Intelligence is a broad field of computer science focused on creating systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence.

Beginner: Grasp the Core Concepts

  • Articles & Tutorials:
    • What is Artificial Intelligence? by John McCarthy - A foundational text from one of the fathers of AI.
    • IBM - What is Artificial Intelligence? - A clear and concise overview of AI concepts.
    • GeeksforGeeks - Introduction to Artificial Intelligence - A comprehensive introduction with examples.
  • Online Course:
    • AI for Everyone by deeplearning.ai on Coursera - A non-technical course perfect for understanding the business and societal impact of AI.
  • YouTube Video:
    • What is Artificial Intelligence? In 5 minutes. - A quick and engaging visual explanation.

Intermediate: Dive Deeper into AI Principles

  • Articles & Tutorials:
    • A Modern Approach to Artificial Intelligence by Stuart Russell and Peter Norvig - The leading textbook in the field, with many chapters available online.
    • AI Topics by AAAI - A collection of articles on various AI topics from the Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence.
    • Distill.pub - In-depth, visual, and interactive articles on machine learning concepts.
  • Online Course:
    • CS50's Introduction to Artificial Intelligence with Python by Harvard University on edX - A hands-on course that teaches you to use machine learning in Python.
  • YouTube Video:
    • MIT 6.034 Artificial Intelligence, Fall 2010 - A full university course available for free.

Expert: Engage with Cutting-Edge Research & Community

  • Research Papers & Journals:
    • arXiv - Artificial Intelligence - Access to the latest research papers in AI.
    • Journal of Artificial Intelligence Research (JAIR) - A leading journal for peer-reviewed AI research.
    • Papers with Code - Artificial Intelligence - Research papers with corresponding code implementations.
  • Community:
    • AAAI (Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence) - A scientific society for AI researchers, with conferences and publications.
  • YouTube Video:
    • Lex Fridman Podcast - In-depth interviews with leading experts in AI and related fields.

Machine Learning (ML)

Machine Learning is a subset of AI that enables systems to learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed.

Beginner: Get Started with ML Basics

  • Articles & Tutorials:
    • Google AI - Machine Learning - A gentle introduction to core ML concepts.
    • Machine Learning for Beginners: An Introduction to Neural Networks - A clear, step-by-step guide.
    • Kaggle - Intro to Machine Learning - Hands-on courses to get you started with practical ML.
  • Online Course:
    • Machine Learning by Stanford University on Coursera (Andrew Ng) - The classic, highly-rated introductory course.
  • YouTube Video:
    • StatQuest with Josh Starmer - Machine Learning Playlist - Breaks down complex ML concepts into easy-to-understand videos.

Intermediate: Build and Deploy ML Models

  • Articles & Tutorials:
    • Hands-On Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn, Keras & TensorFlow by Aurélien Géron - A comprehensive book with practical examples.
    • Towards Data Science on Medium - A platform with a vast collection of articles on ML from practitioners.
    • scikit-learn Documentation and Tutorials - In-depth guides and examples for the popular ML library.
  • Online Course:
    • Machine Learning Engineering for Production (MLOps) Specialization by deeplearning.ai on Coursera - Learn how to deploy ML models in production.
  • YouTube Video:
    • Sentdex - Machine Learning with Python - Practical tutorials on various ML algorithms.

Expert: Contribute to ML Research & Competitions

  • Research Papers & Journals:
    • Journal of Machine Learning Research (JMLR) - A top-tier journal for ML research.
    • NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR Proceedings - Access papers from the leading ML conferences.
    • Distill.pub - For deep, visual explanations of ML research.
  • Community:
    • Kaggle Competitions - Test your skills against the best in the world.
  • YouTube Video:
    • Talks at Google - Machine Learning - Presentations from leading ML researchers and practitioners.

Deep Learning (DL)

Deep Learning is a specialized subfield of machine learning that uses neural networks with many layers (deep neural networks).

Beginner: Understand Neural Networks

  • Articles & Tutorials:
    • Deep Learning Simply Explained by Towards Data Science - A high-level overview.
    • A Beginner's Guide to Neural Networks and Deep Learning by Pathmind - A comprehensive guide for beginners.
    • TensorFlow Playground - Experiment with a neural network right in your browser.
  • Online Course:
    • Deep Learning Specialization by deeplearning.ai on Coursera - A comprehensive and highly-rated specialization.
  • YouTube Video:
    • 3Blue1Brown - But what is a neural network? - An intuitive visual explanation of neural networks.

Intermediate: Master DL Frameworks & Architectures

  • Articles & Tutorials:
    • Deep Learning Book by Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio, and Aaron Courville - The definitive textbook on deep learning.
    • PyTorch Tutorials - Official tutorials for the popular deep learning framework.
    • TensorFlow Tutorials - Official tutorials for the widely-used deep learning library.
  • Online Course:
    • fast.ai - Practical Deep Learning for Coders - A top-down, practical approach to learning deep learning.
  • YouTube Video:
    • MIT 6.S191: Introduction to Deep Learning - A full course from MIT on deep learning.

Expert: Explore Advanced DL Research

  • Research Papers & Journals:
    • arXiv - Deep Learning - The latest research papers in deep learning.
    • Distill.pub's deep learning articles for insightful visualizations of DL concepts.
    • Papers from top conferences like CVPR, ICCV, ECCV (for Computer Vision) and ACL, EMNLP, NAACL (for NLP).
  • Community:
    • The Deep Learning Subreddit (r/deeplearning) - A community for discussing the latest research and trends.
  • YouTube Video:
    • Yannic Kilcher's YouTube Channel - In-depth explanations of cutting-edge research papers.

AI Security

AI Security focuses on securing AI systems from attacks and ensuring their safe and reliable operation.

Beginner: Foundations of AI Security

  • Articles & Tutorials:
    • OWASP Top 10 for Large Language Model Applications - An essential guide to LLM vulnerabilities.
    • Microsoft AI Security Fundamentals - An introductory learning path.
    • An Introduction to Adversarial Machine Learning - A primer on a key area of AI security.
  • Online Course:
    • Introduction to Cybersecurity by University of Washington on edX - Provides a foundational understanding of cybersecurity principles.
  • YouTube Video:
    • What is Adversarial Machine Learning? - A clear and concise explanation of the topic.

Intermediate: Practical AI Security Techniques

  • Articles & Tutorials:
    • MITRE ATLAS (Adversarial Threat Landscape for Artificial-Intelligence Systems) - A knowledge base of adversarial tactics and techniques against AI systems.
    • Google's Secure AI Framework - Best practices for secure AI development.
    • Trail of Bits - AI Security Blog - In-depth articles on various AI security topics.
  • Online Course:
    • Adversarial Machine Learning by University of Oxford - A university course with publicly available materials.
  • YouTube Video:
    • DEF CON AI Village Talks - Presentations on the latest research in AI security from a leading cybersecurity conference.

Expert: Advanced AI Security Research & Community

  • Research Papers & Journals:
    • arXiv - Cryptography and Security - Research papers on the intersection of AI and security.
    • IEEE Symposium on Security and Privacy (S&P), USENIX Security Symposium - Top academic conferences in security often feature AI security research.
    • Distill.pub - "Attacking Machine Learning with Adversarial Examples"
  • Community:
    • AI Village at DEF CON - A community of researchers and practitioners focused on AI security.
  • YouTube Video:
    • Talks from the Workshop on Trustworthy Machine Learning (TrustML) - Presentations from a leading workshop in the field.

Generative AI

Generative AI is a class of AI models that can create new and original content, such as text, images, music, and code.

Beginner: Introduction to Generative AI

  • Articles & Tutorials:
    • What are Generative AI models? by McKinsey - A clear business-focused explanation.
    • A Beginner's Guide to Generative AI by NVIDIA - An easy-to-understand overview.
    • Hugging Face - What is Generative AI? - A practical introduction from a key player in the field.
  • Online Course:
    • Introduction to Generative AI by Google on Coursera - A beginner-friendly course covering the basics.
  • YouTube Video:
    • Generative AI Explained by Google Cloud - A simple and visual explanation.

Intermediate: Deep Dive into Gen AI Models & Architectures

  • Articles & Tutorials:
    • The Illustrated Transformer by Jay Alammar - A visual and intuitive explanation of the architecture behind many generative models.
    • Understanding Diffusion Models: A Unified Perspective by Lilian Weng - A deep dive into the theory behind diffusion models.
    • Hugging Face Diffusion Models Course - A hands-on course to learn and build diffusion models.
  • Online Course:
    • Generative AI with Large Language Models by AWS on Coursera - A course on the theory and practice of building with large language models.
  • YouTube Video:
    • CodeEmporium - Generative AI Tutorials - Practical coding tutorials for various generative AI models.

Expert: Cutting-Edge Generative AI Research & Development

  • Research Papers & Journals:
    • arXiv - Computation and Language - The latest research in natural language processing and generative models.
    • Attention Is All You Need (The Transformer Paper) - The seminal paper that introduced the Transformer architecture.
    • High-Resolution Image Synthesis with Latent Diffusion Models (Stable Diffusion Paper) - A key paper in the field of image generation.
  • Community:
    • Hugging Face Community - A place to collaborate, share models, and discuss the latest in generative AI.
  • YouTube Video:
    • AssemblyAI's YouTube Channel - Features in-depth explanations of the latest generative AI research.
